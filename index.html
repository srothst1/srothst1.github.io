Earthquakes and Urban Areas
San Francisco, CA

Cesium unlock potential for geospatial data. As a city planner, it is important that you undertand the dangers of natural disasters such as earthquakes. While infrequent, earthquakes pose a grave dangers to the vitality of your city. In this article, we explain the causes of earthquakes and we use Cesium to create compelling demos that help you understand which part of your city may be at risk from past natural disasters. While tools such as excell make earthqake data diifuclt to understand and incaccesable, Cesium brings 3d and spacial data to life - helping you to make the most informed decisions possible.

Fault Lines and Earthquakes

Earthquakes often occur near fault lines... One major fault line is the San Andres fault. Thus cities such as San Francsico and Los Angeles that are near the fault lines often have severe earthqueks. Cities such as New York and Philadelphia are further from fault lines and thus do not expiereince as many earthquakes.
